# Project Starter Kit

This is the standard folder structure and file set for launching any new project.

---

## Folder Structure

```text
PROJECT_NAME/
│
├── 00-Overview/
│   ├── README.md
│   ├── Project-Brief.md
│   └── Glossary.md
│
├── 10-Discovery/
│   ├── Context-and-Background.md
│   └── Stakeholder-Notes.md
│
├── 20-Planning/
│   ├── Goals-and-Success-Criteria.md
│   ├── Scope-and-Boundaries.md
│   ├── Initial-Budget.md
│   ├── Timeline-and-Milestones.md
│   └── Risks-and-Assumptions.md
│
├── 30-Execution/
│   ├── Process-Workflow.md
│   ├── SOPs.md
│   └── Task-Backlog.md
│
├── 40-Comms/
│   ├── Meeting-Notes.md
│   └── Status-Updates.md
│
└── 90-Archive/
```

## File Definitions

### README.md
A short overview of the project:
- What the project is about
- Who the project serves
- Current status (idea / planning / active / paused / retired)
- Links to key files in this folder

---

### Project-Brief.md
The project's 1-2 page summary:
- Problem statement
- Proposed solution
- Who benefits and why
- Why this matters now
- High-level scope

---

### Glossary.md
A list of terms and acronyms:
- Technical terms
- Project-specific phrases
- Tribal, cultural, or organizational terms

---

### Context-and-Background.md
Document the origin and setting of the project:
- Where this idea came from
- Past or related projects
- Problems, constraints, or conditions shaping the work

---

### Stakeholder-Notes.md
Capture what matters to the people involved:
- Stakeholder list
- Each stakeholder's goals, concerns, or influence
- Early conversations or quotes

---

### Goals-and-Success-Criteria.md
Define what "good" looks like:
- 3-7 clear goals
- How you'll measure success
- Time horizon for each goal

---

### Scope-and-Boundaries.md
Keep expectations tight and realistic:
- What's *in* scope
- What's *out* of scope
- Key assumptions
- Dependencies you don't control

---

### Initial-Budget.md
An early, rough cost estimate:
- Cost categories (labor, software, supplies, travel, overhead, etc.)
- Estimated dollar amounts
- Notes about uncertainty or quotes needed

---

### Timeline-and-Milestones.md
Sketch the general project arc:
- Major phases
- Estimated dates
- Requirements to mark each milestone "complete"

---

### Risks-and-Assumptions.md
Plan for trouble before it hits:
- Risks
- Likelihood + impact
- Mitigation ideas
- Critical assumptions

---

### Process-Workflow.md
Show how the project actually works:
- Step-by-step process
- Who is responsible at each step
- Inputs and outputs

---

### SOPs.md
Start the formal procedures:
For each SOP:
- Purpose
- When it applies
- Roles involved
- Step-by-step instructions

---

### Task-Backlog.md
A running task list in table form:
- Task name
- Category
- Priority
- Status
- Owner
- Notes

---

### Meeting-Notes.md
Reusable meeting template:
- Date
- Attendees
- Agenda
- Discussion notes
- Decisions
- Action items

---

### Status-Updates.md
Simple status reporting template:
- Time period
- Red / Yellow / Green status
- Highlights
- What's on track
- What's at risk
- Next steps

---
